Air Quality Deterioration and Health Impact During Diwali Festival in India

About Diwali Air Pollution

During Diwali, the festival of lights in India, fireworks significantly contribute to air pollution. The increase in PM2.5 and PM10 levels during this period has led to a rise in respiratory issues and other health problems. Studies show that AQI levels spike dramatically during Diwali week, often reaching hazardous levels (300+ AQI) in major cities like Delhi.
